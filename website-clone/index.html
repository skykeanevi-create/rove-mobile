What is ROVE?

ROVE lets you swap your life with another Gen Z traveler—your place for theirs, your city for theirs. No hotels, no tour buses. Just real life in someone else's world.

  • ✈️ Authentic stays in real homes
  • 🤝 Trusted community of verified swappers
  • 🌍 Discover cities through a local's eyes

How it works

  1. 1

    Create your profile

    Share your place, your vibe, and where you want to go. It only takes a few minutes.

  2. 2

    Find your swap

    Browse destinations and connect with travelers who want to swap with you.

  3. 3

    Confirm & go

    Agree on dates, swap keys (or codes), and get ready to live each other's life.

  4. 4

    Explore like a local

    Arrive, settle in, and experience the city the way they do—with their tips and spots.
